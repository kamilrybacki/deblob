Rationale

The identity makes Deblob feel like governance infrastructure: terse, inspectable, and materially different from indigo developer dashboards.

Metaphor

The mark starts with an irregular blob and resolves through a visible gate into a crystalline schema lattice. That makes the core promise legible without relying on generic database cylinders, AI sparkles, or cloud icons.

The sulfur accent is intentionally sharp: it behaves like a verdict stamp, not a friendly brand highlight. It signals that a decision happened and that the decision can be audited.
