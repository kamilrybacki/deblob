Construction Note

The master mark is built on a 32x32 grid: body bounds x=2-30 and y=5-27, with a flat base near y=26, two unequal lobes, and no pointed apex. The schema core is a rigid 9x9 axis-aligned square at optical center (17,18); clear space is one core width C. Minimum sizes: horizontal 120px, compact 80px, mascot alone 32px, favicon 16px.

Flat-Vector Do / Don't

Do

  • Keep the schema core visible in every mascot mark.
  • Use teal only for approved schema identity.
  • Pair every state with text or a glyph.

Don't

  • No glossy slime, glow, gradient, or chrome treatment.
  • No two-dot face with a straight mouth.
  • No mascot on malformed, quarantine, audit, or CLI error states.
